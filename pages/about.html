Introduction

Welcome to Our Edu Library. We would like to share books in EDU Library which anyone can read them freely. Moreover, it is a non-profit digital library which is easily accessible without account for learning communities to seek books they are interested in as well as which can benefits them educationally, encouraging them to read more books.

Our Vision

Our vision statement describes that all the readers would persue their own goals and future. To create a global, inclusive platform that democratizes access to knowledge by providing fr ee, unlimited access to a diverse collection of books, empowering individuals from all walks of life to learn, grow, and thrive. We envision a world where anyone, regardless of location, background, or economic status, has the opportunity to explore new ideas, enhance their skills, and ignite their imagination through the power of books. Our library aims to foster a culture of lifelong learning, encourage intellectual curiosity, and inspire positive change through the transformative power of reading.

Our Mission

  • Our main mission is that parents, teachers and students can freely download any books they would like to read.
  • We will publish all kinds of books including language learning books, story and comic books and novels written by famous people.
  • We focus on readers to find their own interests and goals for their future lives.
  • We would like to help students, teachers and parents use the information and ideas in their life.
  • We would like to encourage students to develop their independent learning skills.
